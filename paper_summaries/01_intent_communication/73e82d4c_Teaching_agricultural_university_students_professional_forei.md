## Abstract

 Importance.
 The digital transformation of higher education in general, as well as the active integration of artificial intelligence (AI) technologies into foreign language teaching in particular, open up new opportunities for the development of students’ speaking skill, While the use of AI for the development of oral foreign language speaking skills has already been studied on the basis of experiments conducted in linguistic universities, the potential of using chatbots and web applications for the information of professionally oriented dialogic speech of students in non-linguistic universities (especially agricultural ones) remains insufficiently researched to date, which determines the relevance of this work. The aim of the study is to develop a methodology for developing professionally oriented foreign language dialogic speech skills of students in a non-linguistic university based on speaking practice with artificial intelligence tools. To achieve this goal, the following tasks were set: a) to determine the linguistic and methodological features of the Nomi.AI web application; b) to develop a stage-by-stage methodology for developing professionally oriented foreign language dialogic speech skills in students based on additional speaking practice with an artificial intelligence tool; c) to outline the list of developed skills of professionally oriented foreign language dialogic speech of students; d) to test the effectiveness of the developed methodology in a pedagogical experiment.
 
 
 Definition of Concepts.
 The key concepts of this study are: “chatbot’, “web application”, “professionally oriented dialogic speech skills”.
 
 
 Materials and Methods.

 Conclusion.
 The scientific novelty of the research lies in the development and application of an original methodology for developing professional foreign language communication skills based on additional oral speech practice with the Nomi.AI artificial intelligence tool. The obtained data allow us to conclude that the proposed author’s methodology (consisting of seven sequential stages) has generally proven its effectiveness in developing professional foreign language communication skills. Additional extracurricular speech practice with the Nomi.AI web application can contribute to a higher level of development of the above-mentioned skills in agricultural university students compared to traditional teaching. This especially applies to skills that require a more meaningful understanding of the dialogue, competent argumentation and understanding of the interlocutor’s position. At the same time, practice with AI should be exclusively extracurricular in nature and should bot replace classical classroom lessons.
